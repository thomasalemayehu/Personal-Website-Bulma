About Me

I was born in the year 2000 at Yekaiti Hospital Addis Ababa , Ethiopia. Well "few minutes before midnight" they say. My mother tells me that I had good manners before I could tie my shoe laces.

I grew up to the misery of one brother , the oldest and a sister , the middle gold. But in recent years , We have about the norms of Siblings. I'd say even more.

At an early age of 4, I started off at Hill Side School , the same school my previously sister started at. Then, I went on to join Primary school , Middle school and High school at that very same instutuion.By the time I was 18, joined the prestigeous AAiT. Where I'm currently attending.
